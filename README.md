# 🏢 Conference Room Booking Portal

> A clean, full-featured room booking portal built solo during my internship at SKIT's Incubation Cell — completed in under 15 days.

---

## 💡 Why I Built This

Managing conference room availability is one of those problems that sounds simple until you're actually dealing with it. Double bookings, no way to check availability in advance, cancellations with no record — it's a real pain in any organization.

During my internship at SKIT's Incubation Cell, I got the opportunity to build a proper solution for this. I designed and developed the entire portal myself, from scratch, in 15 days.

---

## ✨ What It Does

The portal is built around 3 core workflows:

| Module | What it does |
|---|---|
| 📅 Check Availability | See which rooms are free for a given date and time slot |
| ✅ Book a Room | Reserve a conference room with your name and time details |
| ❌ Cancel Booking | Cancel an existing booking quickly and cleanly |

It also includes multi-location support (Bengaluru & Whitefield branches), an About page, Privacy Policy, and Terms & Conditions — making it feel like a real, production-ready product.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **HTML5** — Page structure and layout
- **CSS3** — Styling and responsive design
- **JavaScript** — Booking logic, availability checks, and DOM interactions

No frameworks, no libraries. Just clean vanilla web tech.
