Frequently Asked Questions

Where is the Whitefield location?

📍 Divyasree Republic of Whitefield, Bangalore, Karnataka 560066. Accessible via Whitefield metro station or taxi.
View on Google Maps →

What facilities are provided?

High-speed Wi-Fi, Projector & Whiteboard, Complimentary Tea & Coffee, AC seating, 24x7 Power Backup, Reception Assistance.

Can I book on an hourly basis?

Yes! All rooms can be booked by the hour. Simply select your preferred time slot after choosing a room.

Is parking available?

Yes, dedicated parking is available at Whitefield. Parking availability may vary — contact us to confirm.

Can I book for multiple days?

Yes! Use the calendar to book rooms on multiple separate days. Each booking is independent.

What safety measures are in place?

Regular sanitization, contactless check-in, and adherence to local health guidelines are strictly followed.
